Orange In "The League"

Syracuse University has a long history of sending players to the NFL. Below is a list of ex-Syracuse football players who took their talents to the next level and are currently in the NFL.

Chandler Jones

Chandler Jones is a defensive end for the Arizona Cardinals. He has been a force in the nfl for a few seasons now, and was named one of the NFL's top 100 players.

Koda Martin '18

Koda Martin is an offensive tackle for the Arizona Cardinals. Koda was an anchor on the offensive line for the 2018 Camping World Bowl champion team. Watch Koda's journey below!

Justin Pugh '12

Justin Pugh is having a great career in the pro's and is starting to get to "league veteran" status. He was a staple in the New York Giant's lineup for a few of quarterback Eli Manning's last NFL seasons. He now plays for the Arizona Cardinals (starting to see a trend yet?)

Sterling Hofrichter '19

Sterling Hofricter is a punter for the Atlanta Falcons. He was known for his deep, high arching punts that would have orange fans "ooh-ing and awwh-ing" in the dome.

Chris Slayton '18

Another member of the beloved 2018 Orange team, Chris Slayton has been with 3 different teams already in his young career in the NFL. He was drafted by the Giants in the 7th round, then signed with the Bills' practice squad in early September of 2020, then at the end of the month was signed by the Falcons practice squad.

Zaire Franklin '17

Zaire Frankin plays linebacker for the Indianapolis Colts. He was a defensive anchor for the Orange, and HC Dino Babers praised him in a press conference, ensuring that whatever team took a chance on him at the next level would not regret it.

Trishton Jackson '18

Trishton Jackson was a transfer for the Orange, from Michigan State. He first saw action for the 'Cuse in the 2018 Bowl Game against West Virginia and caught 3 passes in that game, including one for a 14 yard touchdown. He plays for the Los Angeles Rams.

Riley Dixon '15

Riley Dixon is a punter for the New York Giants. He's a local guy (from nearby Oneida, NY) and helped Syracuse lovingly adopt the moniker "Punter U." Below are some not-so-normal plays from a punter!

Alton Robinson '19

Alton Robinson is a defensive end for the Seattle Seahawks. He was a stud on the defensive side of the ball for the Orange, and is already making some big plays for his new team out west! (See Below)

Cody Conway '18

Cody Conway is an offensive tackle for the Green Bay Packers. He is currently on the practice squad for the Pack.

And more to come! The orange are expected to have some pro-prospects entering the 2021 NFL Draft.
